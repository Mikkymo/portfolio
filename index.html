About Me

Physics Graduate.
Data Analyst.

My journey into data started in a Physics lecture hall. Studying Physics gave me something most analysts don't have from day one: a deep instinct for rigorous analysis, quantitative reasoning, and making sense of complex systems with incomplete information.

Today I apply those same habits to business data — building dashboards, writing SQL queries, and constructing Python analyses that help organisations understand what's happening and why. My work has spanned five industries: sales, CRM, healthcare, telecommunications, finance, and supply chain.

I believe good data analysis is ultimately about communication. A chart no one understands is just noise. I build things people can actually use to make decisions.
